Hello! My name is ScoutYes (J. Kay). I am writing a book called "The Mydanvel" which you can read about below! I love writing stories and get many ideas from books I read such as Septimus Heap and Fablehaven.

The Mydanvel is about two siblings who find out their family has some secrets. They guard the gateway to a copy of Earth where mythical creatures roam, put in there millions of years ago by a group of powerful wizards. We have lived in peace with these creatures for millenia, but now they are thinking of their world as a prison.

I will be live streaming some weekends writing the book! I will also take suggestions and interact with my community (buttons below!)
